PRODUCTION

Mahla Clothing produces small series, bespoke garments and showpieces, all based on demand.

We believe that our customers has the right to know where the products are made, how and by who. All the products are manufactured in Northern Europe, by highly-skilled professionals. Production happens either in our studio in Copenhagen, Denmark or a small sewing atelier in Tallinn, Estonia.

COPENHAGEN

At Mahla Clothing studio in Copenhagen, designs start taking form, and ideas become reality. Design development, pattern design, samples manufacturing, manufacturing of limited editions, small amounts and unique pieces is all taking place at the studio.

We do offer services for making bespoke garments, or made-to-measure clothing as well, everything from sustainable evening gowns to casual wear and accessories.

TALLINN

In the end of 2017, Mahla Clothing started collaboration with a small garment manufacturer located in Estonian capital city, Tallinn. We have been very pleased to work with them ever since.
The company has 15 employees and 24 years of experience behind. Their whole team is very talented, professional and work with a great engagement. They manage the cutting and sewing process of medium size orders.

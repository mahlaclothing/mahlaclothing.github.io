RETURNS


If for any reason you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, you have the possibility of returning the products to us.

You have fourteen (14) days from the date you received your order to return or exchange. If 14 days have passed since your purchase, unfortunately we can’t exchange or offer you a refund.



For making a return, please send us a e-mail to info@mahlaclothing.com with following information;



  • Order number and date
  • Product name
  • Reason for the return

    • To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging. To complete your return, we require a receipt or proof of purchase.

    As a customer, you will be responsible for paying the shipping costs for returning your item. You should consider using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance. We don’t take responsibility for packages lost in transit.

You will receive the refund to the same bank account your order was paid from. Please allow 10 to 12 business days for us to receive and process your return or exchange.
